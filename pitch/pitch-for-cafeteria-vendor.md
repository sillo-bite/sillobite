## 3. Why Existing Solutions Fail

You've probably thought about solutions, but here's why the usual options don't work for canteen vendors like you:

**Traditional POS Machines**
Those expensive hardware setups you see in big restaurants? They cost lakhs to install, require dedicated hardware, take weeks to learn, and still don't solve your core problem: students are still queueing at your counter to place orders. All you've done is digitize the cash register. The chaos remains.

**Food Delivery Apps**
Platforms like Swiggy or Zomato take 20-30% commission per order. That's not sustainable for campus canteens with thin margins. Plus, these apps are built for home delivery, not campus pickup. They don't integrate with your counter operations. And you lose direct control over your customers and pricing.

**Excel Sheets and Manual Tracking**
Some vendors try to maintain registers in Excel or notebooks. This helps with accounting, but it doesn't solve the operational chaos during rush hours. And it's still manual—data entry is time-consuming and error-prone. You're not running a business; you're running a spreadsheet.

**Hiring More Staff**
Adding more counter staff seems logical, but it doesn't scale. More staff means more coordination, more errors, and more salary expenses. The fundamental problem is that your order-taking process is manual. You need to fix the process, not just add more people to a broken system.

**Nothing at All (Status Quo)**
Most canteen vendors just accept that rush hours are chaotic, cash handling is stressful, and operations are always one mistake away from disaster. But that's not a strategy—that's resignation. And it's leaving money on the table every single day.

## 4. Our Solution (SilloBite)

SilloBite is a complete digital ordering and point-of-sale system built specifically for canteen vendors like you.

Here's what it does, in plain language:

**Your Customers Order Digitally**
Students and staff browse your menu on their phones (no app download—it's a website). They place orders, customize items, pay digitally (UPI, cards), and get a notification when their food is ready. They don't have to stand in your queue. They just show up when it's time to pick up.

**You See Every Order Instantly**
The moment a customer places an order, it appears on your dashboard. You see exactly what was ordered, when it needs to be ready, and any special instructions. No more verbal orders. No more mistakes. Just clear, written orders that your kitchen can follow.

**Your Counters Work Like a Team**
If you have multiple counters (one for payment, one for food pickup, one for kitchen display), they're all connected. The payment counter confirms the order. The kitchen counter sees what to prepare. The pickup counter knows when to hand it over. Everything flows smoothly, with real-time status updates.

**Inventory Tracks Itself**
Every time an order is placed, your stock automatically decreases. When you're running low on an item, you get a warning. When something is out of stock, it's instantly disabled on the menu—so customers don't order something you can't deliver. No more guessing, no more surprises.

**You Get Real Dashboards**
At any moment, you can see: how many orders you've taken, how much revenue you've made, which items are selling best, what your average fulfillment time is, and how your day compares to yesterday. At the end of the day (or week, or month), you export a complete report with every transaction logged.

**Settlements Are Automatic**
When it's time to settle accounts with the college, you don't spend hours compiling records. You click a button, generate a full transaction report, and send it. Everything is documented. There are no disputes.

**It Works on Any Device**
You don't need special hardware. Use a tablet, a laptop, or even a phone. The system is web-based and works on anything with a browser. Your staff can learn it in 10 minutes. You're up and running the same day.
